BigBoy

Follow @MeteOzguz

Please keep in mind this is not your typical visual novel, so rating it the same would be unfair. :)

Story

It all starts very abruptly. You’re a man (♂️, 3rd-person POV) thrown into a mission, of which you only know the necessary bits. I like how this very short prologue keeps you in the dark, making it all feel very mysterious. You don’t really get to know much about the other characters yet. There are no choices, just 1 ending. The English is perfectly understandable.

Art

The game only takes up a vertical stroke of the screen. All of the art is in black and white and ‘scrolls’ up a little with every click, along with the speech bubbles.

Just for your information

This is a silent game, there is no background music, nor sound effects. It doesn’t contain sexual content either.
